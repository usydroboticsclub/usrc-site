About Us

University of Sydney Robotics Club (USRC) is a place where USYD students interested in robotics and AI gather to build, share and collaborate! We facilitate entries into robotics competitions such as the Droid Racing Competition (DRC), VIVID Sydney, RobotX and much more. We also run fortnightly workshops/information meetings on everything robotics and AI.

Join us in building the robots of the future!
