USRC Projects for PEP

What's PEP?

PEP is the University of Sydney's Professional Engagement Program. Students are required to spend hours engaging in extracurricular engineering programs to develop skills for industry.

If you're interested in hiring engaged and active students for your organisation, consider sponsoring our events! You'll get to meet some of the most proactive engineering students in Sydney.
